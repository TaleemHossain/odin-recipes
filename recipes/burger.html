Burger Recipe

Description

These black bean burgers are healthy, hearty, and packed with nutritious veggies.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • ½ green bell pepper, cut into 2 inch pieces
  • ½ onion, cut into wedges
  • 3 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon Thai chili sauce or hot sauce
  • ½ cup bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Step 1:

    Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat. Lightly coat a sheet of aluminum foil with cooking spray.

  2. Step 2:

    Mash black beans in a medium bowl with a fork until thick and pasty.

  3. Step 3:

    Finely chop bell pepper, onion, and garlic in a food processor.

    Stir chopped vegetables into mashed beans.

  4. Step 4:

    Combine egg, chili powder, cumin, and chili sauce in a small bowl.

  5. Step 5:

    Stir the egg mixture into mashed bean mixture to combine.

    Mix in bread crumbs until mixture is sticky and holds together.

  6. Step 6:

    Divide mixture into four patties and place on the prepared foil.

  7. Step 7:

    Grill on the preheated grill for about 8 minutes on each side.

    The pattie is done; Arrange the burger and ENJOY!!!
